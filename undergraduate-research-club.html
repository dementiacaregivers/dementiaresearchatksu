Undergraduate Research Club

Students have the opportunity to collaborate on impactful, team-based research projects through the Undergraduate Research Club. This initiative fosters interdisciplinary learning, allowing students to:

  • Gain hands-on experience in dementia-focused research.
  • Work directly with faculty mentors and graduate researchers.
  • Participate in data collection, analysis, and app development.
  • Contribute to scientific publications and conference presentations.

By joining the club, students become an integral part of a meaningful mission to support caregivers of individuals with dementia using AI-powered technology.
