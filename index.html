SmartCare Initiative at Kennesaw State University

Empowering Informal Caregivers with AI Voice Assistance Application

We are building AI-powered mobile app to support dementia caregivers, using participatory research and interdisciplinary collaboration between the Departments of Computer Science and Nursing at KSU.

Explore Our Work

Our Mission

We aim to reduce the burden of dementia caregiving with innovative, community-driven solutions using artificial intelligence.

Meet the Team

Our interdisciplinary team includes faculty, graduate, and undergraduate researchers across Computer Science and Nursing.

Project Description

Discover our two core applications: a dementia-focused smart search engine and an eligibility-checking tool for support programs.
